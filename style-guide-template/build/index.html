Style Guide - Group 08 Project

Purpose/Theme

We decided to follow a corporate theme but giving space to creativity. We expect to have a very well-organized look and give a sense of the viewer of a site that is cohesive and inspire creativity in its presentation. We will use a presentation of the first titles banner text, then we are going to put into boxes as a card view the information presented in the HTML. We want to follow the strategy of “Mobile first” so we can have a great look on a device and then adapt the mobile design into a Desktop one. The creativity in all this formal look will be developed in the use of colors, selections of typography (such as contrasting serif fonts with sans-serif, selecting some decorative fonts for titles, and using proper Sizes according to the hierarchies of the titles, headers and information presented in the page) and some visual effects (such as some shadows and transparency in the cards, banner, and other elements in the page).
